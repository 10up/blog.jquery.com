**jQuery UK is back for 2013!** The jQuery Foundation is pleased to
announce that [White October](http://www.whiteoctober.co.uk/) are
organising another [jQuery UK](http://events.jquery.org/2013/uk)
 conference in Oxford, UK on the 19th of April 2013. There is a [call
for papers](https://jqueryuk2013.busyconf.com/proposals/new) and
[suggestions for
speakers](https://docs.google.com/a/whiteoctober.co.uk/spreadsheet/viewform?formkey=dC14WVhfSjdBMTU0ZUNYR2xpZVdzc3c6MQ#gid=0)are
also welcome. So if you want to take part, or want to see someone at
[jQuery UK](http://events.jquery.org/2013/uk) you know what to do. A
very small number of tickets are [on
sale](http://jquk2013.eventbrite.co.uk/) until the call for papers
finishes on the 11th of October for a “blind bird” price of £130 + VAT.
The full speaker lineup should be announced winter 2012 and the early
bird tickets will go on sale at the same time for £160 + VAT. We've also
listed it on [Lanyrd.com](http://lanyrd.com/2013/jquery-uk/) if you'd
like to follow along there!
